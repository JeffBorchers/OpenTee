How OpenTee Works

Three simple steps to never missing another tee time.

1

Set Your Preferences

Choose your preferred days, times, and courses. Tell us what you're looking for—Saturday mornings at Eagle Eye, or weekday evenings at your local municipal.

2

OpenTee Watches 24/7

Our monitoring system checks availability around the clock. New openings, cancellations, early releases—we catch them all, even while you sleep.

3

Get Notified Instantly

The moment a matching slot appears, you get an SMS or email. One tap to book it yourself, or let OpenTee handle it automatically. Your choice.
