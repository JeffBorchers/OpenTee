Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

The short version: We collect only what's needed to run OpenTee. We don't sell your data. We don't track you around the internet. You can delete your account and all your data anytime.

Who We Are

OpenTee is open-source golf tee time and practice bay booking software developed by OpenTee. We're based in Grand Rapids, Michigan. Our website is opentee.dev.

What Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service:

Account Information

  • Email address — Used for login, notifications, and account recovery
  • Name — Used to personalize your experience
  • Password — Stored as a one-way hash. We never see your actual password

Booking Preferences

  • Preferred courses/facilities — Stores you frequent
  • Preferred times — When you like to play or practice
  • Notification settings — How and when you want alerts

Usage Data

  • Booking history — Tee times and practice bays you've booked
  • Monitor configurations — Booking slots you're watching

Payment Information

We use Stripe for payment processing. Stripe handles your credit card and payment details — we never see or store your full card number. We only store a token that lets us process charges on your behalf.

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Send booking confirmations and reminders
  • Notify you when tee times or practice bays become available
  • Process payments for bookings
  • Improve our service (in aggregate, anonymized ways)
  • Communicate important updates about OpenTee

How We Store and Protect Your Data

Hosted Version

If you use our hosted service at opentee.dev, your data is stored on our servers. We use industry-standard security practices:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — one-way encryption that can't be reversed
  • We limit access to customer data to only those who need it
  • We use Cloudflare for DDoS protection and SSL termination

Self-Hosted Version

OpenTee is open-source under the MIT license. If you self-host, your data stays entirely on your own server. You control everything — we have no access to your installation or your users' data.

Third-Party Services

We use a few third-party services to run OpenTee:

  • Stripe — Payment processing. See Stripe's Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — Security and performance. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy

These providers have their own privacy policies and security practices. We only share with them what's necessary to provide their services.

We Don't Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Period.

Cookies and Tracking

OpenTee uses minimal cookies:

  • Session cookies — Keep you logged in as you navigate the site
  • Preference cookies — Remember your settings (like dark mode)

We do not use:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising cookies or tracking pixels
  • Social media tracking widgets

This may change in the future if we add analytics. If so, we'll update this policy and give you control over tracking.

Your Rights

You have control over your data:

  • Access — Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Correction — Update or correct your information anytime in your account settings
  • Deletion — Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out — Unsubscribe from marketing emails (you'll still receive essential booking notifications)

To exercise these rights, email us at robertmazaborchers@gmail.com or use the account settings in the app.

Data Retention

We keep your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days. Some anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for service improvement.

Children's Privacy

OpenTee is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children. If you're a parent and believe your child has provided us information, contact us and we'll delete it.

International Users

OpenTee is operated in the United States. If you're accessing us from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using OpenTee, you consent to this transfer.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Reach out:
