2. What OpenTee Does

OpenTee is booking software that helps you:

  • Monitor tee time and practice bay availability
  • Get notified when slots open up
  • Book tee times and practice bays at participating facilities

We connect to golf courses and practice facilities through their booking systems. We don't own or operate these facilities — we just help you book with them.

4. How to Use OpenTee (And How Not To)

We built OpenTee to help golfers book tee times. Please use it that way.

Things you can do:

  • Monitor and book tee times for yourself and your group
  • Set up notifications for times you genuinely want
  • Self-host OpenTee and modify it for your needs (MIT license)
  • Contribute improvements back to the open-source project

Things you may NOT do:

  • Use OpenTee to scalp tee times (book and resell at markup)
  • Book tee times you don't intend to use (hoarding slots)
  • Automate bookings at speeds that could harm booking platforms
  • Scrape, reverse-engineer, or attempt to extract data from booking platforms on our behalf without permission
  • Use the Service to compete with golf courses or booking platforms unfairly
  • Share your account credentials with others
  • Use the Service for anything illegal

Important: We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms, especially if your behavior harms golf courses, booking platforms, or other users.

5. No Guarantee of Booking Success

Here's the reality: tee times are limited. Other people want them too. OpenTee monitors availability and helps you book quickly, but we cannot guarantee:

  • You'll get every tee time you want
  • The booking system will always be available
  • Course availability data will always be accurate
  • Your booking will be confirmed (courses can cancel)

OpenTee is a tool, not a promise. Use it to improve your chances, but understand that booking success isn't guaranteed.
